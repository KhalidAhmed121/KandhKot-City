Kandhkot has been making strides in education with a variety of institutions catering to different levels of learning. The city is home to numerous schools and colleges, providing foundational and advanced education to its residents. Notably, the IBA University Kandhkot Campus offers undergraduate programs in disciplines like Business Administration, Computer Science, and Education, aiming to uplift the region through quality higher education. Additionally, the Allama Iqbal Open University has a model study center in Kandhkot, facilitating distance learning for students from diverse backgrounds. These institutions, along with other local schools and colleges, are contributing to the educational development of the area.
